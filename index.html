Welcome

The Transcendence is an organization that recognizes the importance of centering the leadership of Black trans people and prioritizin g the support for Black, Brown, and Indigenous trans women. typically aim to advocate for the rights and well-being of transgender individuals. This includes fighting against discrimination, promoting equal access to healthcare, education, employment, and housing, and raising awareness about the challenges faced by transgender people. we also pro vide support services, resources, and community-building opportunities for transgender individuals and their allies. Additionally, and finaly work to educate the public and policymakers about transgender issues and push for policy changes that advance transgender rights and equality.

Mission

Centering the Leadership of Black Trans People acknowledging the importance of lived experiences in shaping TransLives.

Vision

Celebrating the pride and resilience of the transgender community while advocating for equality and acceptance..

Values

This includes organising TransPride parades, advocacy and education, and also fundraising to relevant causes.
